HOW TO UPLOAD PIXEL CAR RACING TO GAMEHELP.SITE (IONOS)

1. LOG INTO IONOS:
   - Go to https://my.ionos.com
   - Login with your credentials

2. ACCESS YOUR HOSTING:
   - Click "Hosting" or "Webspace"
   - Click "File Manager" or "Dateimanager"

3. UPLOAD FILES:
   - Navigate to the root directory (usually / or /htdocs)
   - Upload these files from /home/leo/pixelcarracing/:
     * pixel-car-racing.html
     * download.html (optional download page)
   
4. ACCESS YOUR GAME:
   - https://gamehelp.site/pixel-car-racing.html
   - OR replace index.html to make it the main page

ALTERNATIVE - FTP UPLOAD:
   Host: ftp.gamehelp.site
   User: [your FTP username from IONOS]
   Pass: [your FTP password]
   
   Then I can upload with:
   curl -T pixel-car-racing.html ftp://ftp.gamehelp.site --user user:pass
